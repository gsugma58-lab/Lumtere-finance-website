Our Mission

To bridge the gap between small businesses and fast, transparent lending without the red tape. We believe every entrepreneur deserves access to fair financing that helps them grow.

Our Vision

To become Papua New Guinea's most trusted financial partner for SMEs and self-employed professionals, known for speed, transparency, and personalized service.

Our Story

Founded on December 25th, 2025, Lumtere Finance was born from a simple observation: traditional banks were too slow and inflexible for small businesses. Our founders, experienced finance professionals themselves, saw countless entrepreneurs struggling to access the capital they needed to grow.

Starting with just a small office in Lae, we've grown to help over 5,000 businesses across Papua New Guinea. We've built our reputation on speed, transparency, and understanding the unique challenges faced by SMEs and self-employed individuals.

Today, we're proud to be one of the fastest-growing small finance companies in the region, but our mission remains the same: provide fast, fair financing without the hassle.
